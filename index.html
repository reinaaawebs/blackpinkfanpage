Lisa (Lalisa Manoban)

Lisa, born as Lalisa Manoban, is the main dancer and rapper of Blackpink. Hailing from Thailand, she brings a unique blend of charisma, talent, and international appeal to the group. Lisa's dynamic dance moves, rap skills, and magnetic personality have made her a global sensation, earning her millions of fans around the world.

Rosé (Park Chaeyoung)

Rosé, whose birth name is Park Chae-young, is the main vocalist of Blackpink. She is known for her soulful voice, emotional depth, and remarkable talent as a musician. Rosé's passion for music shines through in her performances, captivating audiences with her powerful vocals and heartfelt expressions.

Jennie (Jennie Kim)

Jennie, born as Jennie Kim, is a rapper and vocalist in Blackpink. With her unique sense of style and charismatic stage presence, she has become a fashion icon and trendsetter in the K-pop industry. Jennie's confident and assertive demeanor adds an electrifying energy to Blackpink performances.

Jisoo (Kim Ji-soo)

Jisoo, whose full name is Kim Ji-soo, is a member of Blackpink known for her powerful vocals and captivating stage presence. She exudes elegance and grace both on and off stage, earning her the title of "Miss Korea" among fans. Jisoo's warm and caring personality makes her beloved by her bandmates and fans alike.
